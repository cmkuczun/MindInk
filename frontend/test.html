How do your inputs work together to generate your art?

Subject Matter

This tells the model what to generate. It is the main part of the prompt.

Art Style

This input tells the model how to generate your image. Whether you want a photograph-like image or something that looks like it was made using origami, the model will generate your subject matter adhering to this choice!

Color Scheme

If you're feeling fun and want the model to create a colorful image or you have a favorite color that you want your image to be, your needs are met through this color selection.
